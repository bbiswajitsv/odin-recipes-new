Chicken Curry

Description:

Chicken curry is a traditional Indian dish made by simmering chicken pieces with plenty of spices, herbs, onions and tomatoes. It is believed to have originated in India and now enjoyed all over the world.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (about 700g) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger, garlic, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to absorb.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add coriander powder and cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate.
  6. Add the marinated chicken to the pan. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is browned.
  7. Lower the heat, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook for another 15-20 minutes until it's tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  8. Add garam masala and adjust salt and spice levels according to your taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves. Serve hot with rice or bread of your choice.
